One-shot panorama with a spoon

Step 1
Get a "ladle" (a soup serving spoon) which is reflective on the convex part.




Step 2
Bend the handle backwards to make it easier to hold above a camera.

Step 3
Set up a camera which points up vertically.

Step 4
Hold the convex part of the spoon above the camera's lens.

Step 5
Take a picture.



Step 6
Transform the polar image to a panoramic image.
